About Karo Pitch

Built so strong founders are not limited by location.

Thousands of founders are building strong businesses in D2C, consumer, MSME, manufacturing, and tech. Many still lack direct access to investors and mentorship.

Karo Pitch was created to bridge this gap through a structured, investor-ready platform where selected startups present their business and get the visibility they deserve.

What it is

A discovery and pitch platform connecting high-potential founders with active investors.

Why now

Founder quality is rising in Tier-2 and Tier-3 India, but investor access is still uneven.

Mission

Make startup funding opportunities accessible to builders across Bharat.

How It Works

A simple 4-step pitch journey

Step 1

Apply with your pitch deck

Submit your startup details, business model, and growth metrics.

Step 2

Get shortlisted by KaroStartup

Applications are reviewed for clarity, traction, and investor relevance.

Step 3

Pitch live to investors

Present in a curated pitch room and get direct investor interaction.

Step 4

Raise funding and scale

Move from pitch to partnership with long-term growth support.
